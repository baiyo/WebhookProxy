Why use WebhookProxy?

  • Easy to Integrate Simply replace discord.com with webhook.bayo.moe in your webhook URL.
  • Tried and Tested Reliably serving webhooks with built-in monitoring and uptime.
  • Kept Up Maintained long-term (maybe) even if Discord changes Roblox webhook support.
  • Complies with ToS Built with Discord's Terms of Service in mind, including ratelimit enforcement and anti-abuse measures.
  • Ratelimit Handling We queue requests and enforce Discord's ratelimits to prevent disruptions. Excessive requests may lead to temporary bans, so please use responsibly.

Usage

Replace your Discord webhook URL:

https://discord.com/api/webhooks/...
→
https://webhook.bayo.moe/api/webhooks/...

⚠️ Respect Discord's ratelimits (5 requests per 2 seconds). Invalid payloads may trigger stricter limits.
